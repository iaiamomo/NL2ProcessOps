The manufacturing process of spindles in HSD company is fully automated. When a new order for a spindle arrives at the sales department, a new process instance is initiated. The warehouse system retrive the necessary raw materials, and in parallel the L12 line is set up for the assembly of the ordered spindle. Once the warehouse successfully retrieves the raw materials and the L12 line is set up, the spindle is assembled over the L12 line. Subsequently, the spindle undergoes testing and running-in in the smart tester. If the outcome of the test is negative, the spindle is sent to maintenance. Then, the the process ends.
